Privacy Policy for Yuna-TT

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Yuna-TT collects, uses, and protects information when you use our TikTok Shop content management tool.

2. Information We Collect

Yuna-TT accesses the following data through TikTok's API:

  • Public video metadata (view counts, engagement metrics)
  • Account information for content scheduling

Yuna-TT does not collect or store personal data from third parties.

3. How We Use Information

All data accessed by Yuna-TT is used solely for internal content management purposes, including video scheduling, caption generation, and engagement monitoring.

4. Data Sharing

Yuna-TT does not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

5. Data Security

Yuna-TT implements appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to data.
